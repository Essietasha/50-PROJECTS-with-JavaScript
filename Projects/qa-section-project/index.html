How do I create an account?

  1. Open the app and click on the "Sign Up" button.
  2. Enter your email address, create a password, and fill in the required information.
  3. Click on "Create Account" to complete the registration process.
  4. You will receive a confirmation email. Click on the link in the email to verify your account.

How can I reset my password?

  1. Go to the login screen and click on "Forgot Password?"
  2. Enter your registered email address.
  3. Check your email for a password reset link and follow the instructions.

How do I update my profile information?

  1. Log in to your account.
  2. Go to the "Profile" section by clicking on your profile icon.
  3. Click on "Edit Profile."
  4. Update the information you want to change.
  5. Click "Save" to apply the changes.

How do I contact customer support?

  1. Go to the "Settings" or "Help" section in the app.
  2. Click on "Contact Us" or "Customer Support."
  3. You can either send an email to support@support.com or use the in-app chat feature.
  4. Provide details about your issue, and our support team will get back to you as soon as possible.li>
